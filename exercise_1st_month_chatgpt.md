# DAILY WARM-UP (6–8 min)

## Cat–Cow
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/89/Cat_Cow_Yoga_Asana.png" width="420"/>

- Move slowly between rounded and arched spine  
- 6 controlled breaths

---

## Scapular Push-Ups
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2b/Scapular_push-ups.png" width="420"/>

- Arms straight  
- Only shoulder blades move  
- 2 × 8 reps

---

## Glute Bridges
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/33/Glute_bridge_exercise.png" width="420"/>

- Drive through heels  
- Squeeze glutes at top  
- 12 reps (2s hold)

---

## Active Hang Prep
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/88/Active_hang_pullup.png" width="420"/>

- Hang from beam  
- Pull shoulders down (arms straight)  
- 2 × 15 sec

---

# WORKOUT A  
**Push · Squat · Core · Shoulder**

---

## Push-Ups
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/9d/Pushups.gif" width="420"/>

- Body straight  
- Elbows ~30–45°  
- 2–3 × 8–12 reps  
- Progress: slower descent

---

## Bodyweight Squats
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a4/Air_squat.png" width="420"/>

- Feet shoulder-width  
- Sit back and down  
- 3 × 10–15 (3s down)

---

## Plank
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8b/Plank_exercise.png" width="420"/>

- Ribs tucked  
- Glutes tight  
- 3 × 20–40 sec

---

## Calf Raises
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7d/Standing_calf_raise.png" width="420"/>

- Rise fully onto toes  
- Slow control  
- 3 × 15–20

---

## Shoulder External Rotation (Bottle)
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4b/Shoulder_external_rotation_with_band.png" width="420"/>

- Elbow tucked  
- Rotate outward slowly  
- 2 × 12 / arm

---

# WORKOUT B  
**Pull · Hinge · Lunge · Core · Shoulder**

---

## Passive Hang
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6c/Dead_hang_pull-up.png" width="420"/>

- Relaxed hang  
- 20–30 sec

---

## Active Hang
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/88/Active_hang_pullup.png" width="420"/>

- Shoulder blades down  
- Arms straight  
- 15–25 sec

---

## Scapular Pull-Ups
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1c/Scapular_pullups.png" width="420"/>

- Small motion  
- No elbow bend  
- 5–10 reps

---

## Negative Pull-Ups
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0c/Negative_pull-up.png" width="420"/>

- Start at top  
- Lower 4–6 sec  
- 2–4 reps

---

## Hip Hinge (Good Morning)
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6e/Good_morning_exercise.png" width="420"/>

- Push hips back  
- Neutral spine  
- 3 × 12–15

---

## Reverse Lunges
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/Reverse_lunge.png" width="420"/>

- Step back  
- Knee softly touches floor  
- 3 × 8–12 / leg

---

## Side Plank
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1c/Side_plank.png" width="420"/>

- Straight line  
- Shoulder stacked  
- 20–35 sec / side

---

## Y–T–W Shoulder Raises
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0f/YTWL_shoulder_exercises.png" width="420"/>

- Face down  
- Slow controlled raises  
- 2 rounds × 6 each

---

## Deep Squat Hold
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7c/Deep_squat.png" width="420"/>

- 30–60 sec

## Hip Flexor Stretch
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/e4/Hip_flexor_stretch.png" width="420"/>

- 30 sec / side

---

## Thoracic Open Book
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3b/Open_book_stretch.png" width="420"/>

- 6 slow reps / side

---

## Shoulder CARs
<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/9e/Shoulder_CARs.png" width="420"/>

- Large slow circles  
- 3 reps / side

---

# WEEK 4 TEST DAY

- Max clean push-ups (leave 1 rep)
- Longest active hang
- Smooth negatives
- Optional strict pull-up (pain-free only)
